Parallax Website

Section One

"Parallax scrolling is a web design technique that creates an immersive experience by making background elements move at a different speed than foreground content. This adds depth and visual appeal, making websites feel dynamic and engaging. As users scroll, layers shift smoothly, creating an illusion of motion and storytelling through design. Parallax is widely used in modern web design for landing pages, portfolios, and interactive websites. It enhances user engagement, improves aesthetics, and provides a unique browsing experience. By combining CSS and JavaScript, designers can create stunning effects that bring websites to life, making them more memorable and interactive."
